Executive Chef - Fine Dining Restaurant

ABOUT THE ROLE:
We are seeking an experienced Executive Chef to lead our culinary team at our award-winning fine dining restaurant. The ideal candidate will have a passion for creating exceptional dining experiences, a strong culinary background, and excellent leadership skills to manage and inspire our kitchen staff.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
• Create and develop innovative seasonal menus that showcase local ingredients
• Oversee all kitchen operations, including food preparation, cooking, and presentation
• Maintain high standards of food quality, taste, and presentation
• Manage kitchen staff, including hiring, training, and performance evaluations
• Control food costs, inventory, and waste management
• Ensure compliance with health, safety, and sanitation regulations
• Collaborate with the restaurant manager and front-of-house staff
• Source high-quality ingredients from local suppliers and build relationships with vendors
• Participate in menu tastings and special events

REQUIREMENTS:
• 7+ years of culinary experience, with at least 3 years in a leadership role
• Culinary degree or equivalent professional training
• Strong knowledge of various cooking techniques, ingredients, and culinary trends
• Experience in menu planning, food costing, and inventory management
• Excellent leadership and team management skills
• Strong communication and interpersonal abilities
• Ability to work under pressure in a fast-paced environment
• Knowledge of health, safety, and sanitation regulations
• Creativity and passion for culinary arts

PREFERRED SKILLS:
• Experience in fine dining establishments
• Knowledge of wine pairing and beverage programs
• Experience with farm-to-table cooking and sustainable practices
• Familiarity with international cuisines
• Food styling and presentation skills
• Experience with special dietary requirements (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
• Computer skills for inventory and ordering systems

BENEFITS:
• Competitive salary based on experience
• Health, dental, and vision insurance
• Paid time off
• Meal allowance
• Professional development opportunities
• Employee discounts
• Potential for profit sharing
